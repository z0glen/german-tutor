Inspiration.

While I was in Germany, I found myself completely surrounded by the language. I needed a way to keep track of the new words and phrases that I saw while walking around or reading a book. I started with simple flashcards, but quickly realized that I needed a better way to store all the information I wanted to remember. I started working on language-tutor and it proved to be invaluable on my quest to learn German.

Future Directions.

I have lots of ideas on how to improve language-tutor. I want to be able to edit the words after I've entered them, add more options for practicing (like fill in the blank for phrases or article tests for words), and I want to integrate a translation API, so that I don't need to look up the meaning on my own.
